## Prose rule: no em dash characters

When writing user-facing copy, docs, test descriptions, or validation artifacts, avoid Unicode dash punctuation characters used as em/en dashes (for example U+2014, U+2013, and U+2015). Prefer a plain hyphen, parentheses, or a sentence rewrite.
